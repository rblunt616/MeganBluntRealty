Growing up in St. Charles...

Growing up in the small town of St. Charles, Michigan, as the youngest of six children, do it yourself projects became a staple of my childhood. Everyone in our family learned to pitch in, whether it was a smaller project like painting a room (or 10) or a larger endeavor like adding an entire second story to our home! Throughout my childhood working alongside my parents and siblings on these DIY projects, I learned how important a home is in bringing a family together. This a value that I carry with me today, as I help clients find the perfect place to call home.  

Later on...

My husband and I began house hunting during our engagement and purchased our first home in Swartz Creek. That tiny starter home would serve as the venue for many memories. We even ended up getting married in the living room, in front of a small group of family and friends! Continuing in the tradition I learned growing up, we took on many DIY projects in our new home. While learning first hand about “sweat equity” through the many projects we took on at home, I also juggled helping my husband open a new Red Wing Shoe Store, while continuing to work in early childhood education myself.

Now...

As time marched on, our family grew as we welcomed our children into the world. Today, we make our home in Midland with our two boys. Midland has always felt like home to me in many ways, as my family has roots in the community dating back to the late 1800’s. My childhood is filled with many wonderful memories of visiting my great-grandparents home here in town and running around playing in the backyard with my cousins. I’ve been so proud to bring my family back to Midland to make our home, and I am thrilled for the opportunity to help my clients find the perfect place for their family to call home.
